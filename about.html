About

A few things you should know

"To know your purpose, find your happiness"

Growing up, I always believed that my career would involve sitting in front of a computer since I was so fascinated by the things that it could do. C and C++ during my school days steered me towards programming. Even though I studied Electronics and Communication Engineering as an undergraduate, I was particularly interested in courses that involved programming.

Because of my passion for programming since high school and college, I was persuaded to take a job offer from Infosys Private Limited. I received full stack developer training, which includes Python, SQL, mongo dB, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node JS, Express framework, and React JS, and received a high performer tag. The beginning phase of the project was intimidating because I was completely new to Angular and had to deal with a large multi-tenant codebase. The tasks I was given were uncommon in that they required proof of concept and groundwork to perform. These challenges made programming more complex and intriguing for me, and I learned the principles and methods behind numerous concepts as a result of them. I could grasp how the entire UI code works in less than four months and was confident handling crucial duties and complicated modules of the application. With 3 years of work experience as a full stack developer I have built a self-discipline of developing efficient, quality, and reusable code.

Currently I am pursuing my Master's in Information Technology Management at UTD to develop a strong foundation for my future career and my time at Infosys gave me a fondness for IT.

I would describe myself as follows: If something fascinates me, I will give it my all. I prefer unconventional methods and wind up exploring a lot of concepts in the process, resulting in a beneficial outcome that has always delighted me. Whenever I encounter blank pages, the drive to fill them arises because doing so will be worth every second I put in.
